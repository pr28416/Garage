              Contact
            

People
Please contact the DUS team at cs-dus@seas.harvard.edu if you have any questions about declaring a CS concentration, study abroad, taking CS 91r, or other questions.


Prof. Boaz Barak, co-Director of Undergrad Students (DUS), cs-dus@seas.harvard.edu


Prof. Stephen Chong, co-Director of Undergrad Students (DUS), cs-dus@seas.harvard.edu


Dr. Adam Hesterberg, Assistant Director of Undergrad Students (ADUS), cs-dus@seas.harvard.edu
Please contact Adam if you have questions about the CS concentration, CS opportunities, and CS community events


Beth Musser, Undergraduate Program Coordinator (UPC), cs-dus@seas.harvard.edu, (617) 496-0789
Please contact Beth regarding routine CS questions, CS advising programming, and other aspects of the undergraduate computer science experience. If you don’t know who to contact, Beth can help Harvard undergraduates to find the right person.


Outside the DUS Team


Amber Meyers, Program Manager for Student Industry Experience - Computer Science, ameyers@seas.harvard.edu 
Amber offers programming to help you explore and navigate experiential learning and careers in Computer Science.


Sarah Colgan, Undergraduate Academic Programs Administrator, scolgan@seas.harvard.edu, (617) 496-1524
Sarah can help you with questions regarding your Course Assistant or Teaching Fellow position.


Abby Rahn, Program Administer in the Office of Academic Programs, abbyrahn@seas.harvard.edu 
Abby can help you with questions regarding the SM side of the SEAS AB/SM program. Please refer to the AB/SM page for application information and FAQs, and keep in mind that you may be referred to the Office of Undergraduate Education, Graduate School of Arts and Sciences, the Registrar’s Office, and/or your Allston Burr Resident Dean for questions regarding non-SEAS policies.
